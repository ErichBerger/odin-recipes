Raicilla Siesta

A fun, fruity drink with a hint of smoke.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz Raicilla
  • 1/2 oz campari
  • 1/2 oz lime juicep
  • 1/2 oz grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup

Steps:

  1. Put ice in a shaker.
  2. Pour ingredients over ice.
  3. Close shaker and shake for 20 seconds.
  4. Strain drink into a martini glass.
  5. (Optional) Garnish with a lime twist.
