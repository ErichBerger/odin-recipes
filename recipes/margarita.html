Margarita

Sweet, easy to drink, citrusy goodness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz silver tequila
  • 1 oz orange liqueur
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • optional: salt

Directions:

  1. Salt the rim. Run lime around glass, dip rim.
  2. Add ice, tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice to container and stir.
  3. Shake cocktail shaker for 10 seconds.
  4. Strain into margarita glass and serve with lime wedge.
