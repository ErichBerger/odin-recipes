Old Fashioned

A smoky, sweet, caramel-y drink.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 oz bourbon
  • orange twist

Directions:

  1. Add the sugar and bitters into a mixing glass, then add the water, and stir until the sugar is nearly dissolved.
  2. Fille the mixing glass with ice, add bourbon, stir.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass over one large ice cube.
  4. Express the oil of an orange twist over the glass.
